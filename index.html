About Me

I’m a healthcare operations and reporting professional with experience improving administrative workflows, financial visibility, and patient access. My background includes modernizing a paper-based rural medical practice, implementing practical systems for scheduling, billing, bookkeeping, and reporting, and helping scale operations across multiple locations. I’m especially interested in the intersection of analytics, compliance, and operations, where clear processes and good data can directly improve how organizations function. I tend to do my best work in environments that need structure, problem-solving, and someone who can turn messy day-to-day operations into sustainable systems.
